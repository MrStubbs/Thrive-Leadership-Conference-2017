Finances & Sales Management

Hello! Salut! My name is James Wall and I am currently a Grade 12 student in Year 2 of the IB Diploma Programme. My experience in managing school finances primarily comes from serving as TMS School’s Student Council treasurer for the 2016-17 academic year, as well as treasurer for both my high school and elementary school’s fundraising clubs. Also holding a part-time job throughout the year, wisdom in money management is a constant consideration. Leading our Finance and Sales Management seminar, I’ll guide us through thorough discussions on effective budgeting, financial record keeping, cash flow, and sales management. I am looking forward to in-depth discussions and collaboratively strategizing on how to be in the black!

Social Development

Hi! My name is Reyana Lalani, and I am a grade 12 student at TMS. I have been involved in Student council for six years now, as a class rep, a house captain, and now as the Head of Houses. In addition to Student council, I am the President of DECA, and the Vice President of Model UN. In my free time, I teach a grade one math class, and run my own tutoring business where I work one on one with students between the ages of 5-15 to strengthen their math and English skills. Each of my individual leadership roles has allowed me to gain knowledge and experience on communication, inclusivity, teamwork, and leading and encouraging both small and large groups of people of all ages. I will be co-leading the social development conference, where we will be focusing on inclusivity and culture, student involvement, and House systems within schools. I look forward to sharing my experiences, and hearing from anyone involved in my conference!

Public Relations

Hello! My name is Madison Muzzo, and I am currently a grade 11 student. This year I am serving as Communications Representative for the TMS Student Council and Co-Captain of our Varsity Girls Field Hockey team. In both of these positions, driving success factors include prominent leadership skills as well as intimate communication networks amongst varying groups of people. The skills I have developed in these facets are imperative in allowing me to successfully facilitate healthy relationships and positive outlooks amid the student body towards student council. Alongside Achu Mukundan, I will be co-leading the Public Relations seminars, allowing us, as a whole, to explore what public relations means and how we can address public relations related issues in our respective school communities. I look forward to sharing with you and hearing your insights!

Hi! Bonjour! My name is James Wall and I am currently a Grade 12 student in Year 2 of the IB Diploma Programme. As TMS School’s Head Boy, and predecessor to my co-leader, Andrea Chang, as leader of our fundraising club for social justice and environmental concerns, effectively managing all members of a team is critical to facilitating the collective flow of ideas necessary to be productive. Despite personally running TMS School’s Student Council meetings via consensus decision making, our school has a history of different group organizational structures for meetings and executing events, including Harkness discussion circles, hierarchical organization, and vote-based decision making. We’ll discuss the pros and cons of these strategies, as well as effective implementation of them. Andrea and I look forward to meeting all of you for some fruitful, collaborative discussion!

Creative Problem Solving
